Privacy Policy for ScaleChef

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Information We Collect

ScaleChef does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All recipe data you enter is stored locally on your device only.

No Data Collection

  • We do not collect personal identifiers (name, email, phone)
  • We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not share any data with third parties

In-App Purchases

Premium features are processed through Apple's App Store using StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all purchase transactions.

URL Import Feature

When you use the URL import feature, the app fetches the webpage directly from your device. We do not proxy or store any web content on our servers.

Data Storage

All your recipes and preferences are stored locally on your device using CoreData. No data is sent to external servers. You can delete all data by removing the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

ScaleChef is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
